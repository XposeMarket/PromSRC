20: | `[MEMORY]` MEMORY.md | ~500–2,000 | 8k char cap in interactive; capped at 5k in team_subagent |
21: | `[BUSINESS]` BUSINESS.md | ~200–1,000 | only when business_context_mode is on |
22: | `[TODAY_NOTES]` intraday | ~100–500 | interactive + team_manager only; grows through the day |
23: | `[PROJECT_CONTEXT]` | ~100–500 | only when session is project-bound |

26: | Skills hint + active skills | ~200–300 | [SKILLS] playbook count + [ACTIVE_SKILLS] recently used |
27: | `[CIS_CONTEXT]` | ~150–300 | entity-aware business profile |
