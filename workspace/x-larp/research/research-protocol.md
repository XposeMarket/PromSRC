# X LARP Research Protocol

## Daily Research Workflow

The **X-Research Agent** scans X daily (typically 8:00 AM) to identify:
- Trends relevant to AI, automation, and Prometheus capabilities
- Strategic opportunities for persona content
- Questions and discussions the team should engage with
- Competitor or adjacent product mentions
- Community sentiment and feedback

---

## Research Scanning Scope

### Primary Topics
- AI automation and workflow tools
- Agent systems and autonomous agents
- Desktop application innovations
- API integration and tool-chaining
- Prompt engineering and advanced techniques
- AI safety, limitations, and honest discussions

### Secondary Topics
- Relevant tool comparisons (Claude, OpenAI, Grok, etc.)
- Business automation and productivity
- Developer tools and DX improvements
- Emerging AI products and announcements

### Out-of-Scope
- Unrelated news (sports, entertainment, politics unless directly tied to AI/tech)
- Low-quality discourse or engagement bait
- Topics requiring immediate news-cycle response (handled by coordinator)

---

## Daily Research Output

Each morning research scan produces:
- **research-log-YYYY-MM-DD.md**: Full scan results with links, topics, key quotes
- **opportunities.md**: Prioritized list of content opportunities for each persona
- **engagement-targets.md**: Recommended accounts or threads to engage with

---

## Red Flags & Escalations

Research stops and escalates to coordinator if:
- Potential legal issue or misinformation detected
- Offensive or toxic content trends upward
- Prometheus product mentioned negatively or inaccurately
- Breaking news requiring immediate response
- Competitor announcements or major market shift

---

## Research Agent Responsibilities

1. **Daily Scan** (8:00 AM): Comprehensive X scan of all primary topics
2. **Opportunity Identification**: Classify findings by persona and urgency
3. **Verification**: Double-check any claims before flagging as content opportunity
4. **Documentation**: Store all findings in daily research log
5. **Escalation**: Flag red flags and breaking news to coordinator immediately
6. **Archive**: Maintain searchable historical record of scanned topics and trends

---

## Coordinator Integration

After research completes:
1. Coordinator reviews opportunities and escalations
2. Coordinator approves 1-2 high-priority content angles
3. Coordinator assigns to persona and adds to engagement calendar
4. Persona drafts content from research context
5. Coordinator reviews draft and approves or requests revision
