# X LARP Team Coordinator Instructions

## Role Overview
The team coordinator acts as the operational manager for the Prometheus X LARP accounts system. Your responsibilities span research oversight, persona management, content approval, engagement scheduling, and safeguard monitoring.

---

## Daily Responsibilities

### Morning (8:00 AM)
1. Review X-Research Agent output from overnight scan
2. Identify 1-2 high-priority content opportunities
3. Assign opportunities to personas with context
4. Update engagement calendar with research-triggered posts

### Midday (12:00 PM)
1. Review incoming persona drafts
2. Check drafts against x-larp-posting-guidelines
3. Approve or request revisions
4. Confirm posting times with drafts

### Afternoon/Evening
1. Monitor live posts and initial engagement
2. Flag any deviations from guidelines
3. Track engagement metrics (likes, replies, follows)
4. Respond to coordinator alerts or escalations

### Weekly (Friday 4:00 PM)
1. Generate weekly engagement summary
2. Review account health metrics
3. Adjust engagement calendar if needed
4. Archive completed posts and research logs

---

## Approval Workflow

### For Every Draft
1. **Tone Check**: Does voice match persona profile and guidelines?
2. **Accuracy Check**: Are all claims verifiable? Does research support them?
3. **Guideline Check**: No forbidden content types? Follows engagement rules?
4. **Cross-Account Check**: No duplication with other personas' recent posts?
5. **Timing Check**: Does posting time align with calendar and strategy?

### Approval Decision
- **Approve**: Post at scheduled time
- **Request Revision**: Explain specific issue and request resubmission
- **Reject**: Only if content violates guidelines; suggest alternative approach

---

## Escalation Handling

### Red Flags That Require Immediate Action
- False capability claims
- Hostile or disrespectful engagement
- Posting outside authorized times
- Potential security or legal issues
- Account anomalies (unexpected followers, unusual metrics)

### Escalation Process
1. **Pause**: Stop any pending posts from the persona
2. **Investigate**: Gather context and evidence
3. **Report**: Document issue in `workspace/x-larp/incidents.md`
4. **Resolve**: Work with persona to correct and reapprove
5. **Archive**: Keep records for review and learning
