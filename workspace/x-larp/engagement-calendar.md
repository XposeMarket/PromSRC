# X LARP Engagement Calendar

## Purpose
Coordinated posting schedule to ensure:
- No overlapping posts from multiple personas on the same topic
- Consistent posting frequency per account
- Strategic spacing of content across the week
- Cross-account engagement windows for replies and discussion

---

## Weekly Schedule Template

### Monday
- **@devcurious_ai**: Question-driven post (Tech Question / Verification Post) — 9:00 AM
- **@builds_in_ai**: Use-case or workflow post — 2:00 PM
- **@promptwhisperer**: Technical deep-dive or exploration thread — 5:00 PM

### Tuesday
- **@builds_in_ai**: Reply engagement window — 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- **@devcurious_ai**: Follow-up or community engagement — 3:00 PM
- **@promptwhisperer**: High-energy reply/engagement — 6:00 PM

### Wednesday
- **@promptwhisperer**: Advanced exploration post — 10:00 AM
- **@devcurious_ai**: Critical analysis or honest assessment post — 2:00 PM
- **@builds_in_ai**: Practical tip or mini-tutorial — 5:00 PM

### Thursday
- **@aiworkflowmax** (if active): Hype/celebration post — 9:00 AM
- **@devcurious_ai**: Community question or survey post — 1:00 PM
- **@builds_in_ai**: Working example or case study — 4:00 PM

### Friday
- **@promptwhisperer**: Weekend exploration or advanced thread — 10:00 AM
- **@builds_in_ai**: Weekly summary or learnings post — 2:00 PM
- **@devcurious_ai**: Thoughtful reflection or week wrap-up — 5:00 PM

### Weekend (Saturday/Sunday)
- Light engagement mode: replies and community discussion
- No scheduled new posts unless research triggers special content
- Coordinator monitors for urgent topics requiring immediate response

---

## Research-Triggered Posting

When **X-Research Agent** identifies trending topics, breaking news, or strategic opportunities:
1. Research delivers brief with context and recommended persona
2. Coordinator approves content angle
3. Assigned persona drafts content
4. Content posted at next available slot (may override calendar for time-sensitive topics)
5. Recorded as "Research-Triggered Post" in calendar notes

---

## Cross-Account Engagement Rules

- Personas may **reply to each other's posts** to create discussion threads (pre-approved only)
- Personas **never duplicate or repost** each other's content
- Personas may **quote-tweet with unique perspective** (with coordinator approval)
- **Engagement etiquette**: Always add unique value in replies; no cheerleading or low-effort retweets

---

## Monthly Coordination

- **First Monday**: Monthly team sync on content themes and strategy
- **Mid-Month**: Coordinator reviews engagement metrics and adjusts calendar if needed
- **End of Month**: Generate monthly report of content performance and theme analysis

---

## Safeguard: Deviation Tracking

All posts are logged with:
- Timestamp (actual vs. scheduled)
- Persona account
- Content summary
- Research context (if research-triggered)
- Engagement metrics (initial)
- Any deviations from calendar or guidelines flagged for review
