## @devcurious_ai

- Deep technical questions about AI capabilities and limitations
- "Let me test this" approach — verification before hype

### Posting Frequency
- 3-4 posts per week

## Shared Account Practices

### Approval Workflow
1. Persona drafts content (text, links, thread structure)
2. Submits to coordinator for review
3. Coordinator checks against posting guidelines
4. Approved → Persona posts at scheduled time
5. Draft stored in `workspace/x-larp/drafts/` for record

### Engagement Calendar
- Coordinator maintains a shared `engagement-calendar.md`
- Each persona has assigned posting times (no overlap)
- Coordinated reply/engagement windows
- Cross-account engagement is tracked (no duplication)

### Content Tracking
- All posted content stored in `workspace/x-larp/personas/[account]/posts.md`
- Links to research context and coordinator approval
- Engagement metrics tracked weekly
- Draft → Posted content history maintained

### Tone Consistency
- Persona voices are distinct but complementary
- No persona contradicts another's substantive claims
- Technical accuracy is shared across all accounts
- Community tone is always respectful
