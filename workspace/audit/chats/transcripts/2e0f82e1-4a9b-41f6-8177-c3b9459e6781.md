### Goal of the skill
A reusable workflow that lets me:
1. **find leads**
2. **qualify them**
3. **enrich them**
4. **score/prioritize them**
5. **output them in a usable format**
6. optionally **draft outreach angles**

### What we should define before building it
We need to lock in:

- **Target business type**
  - local service businesses?
  - restaurants?
  - medical?
  - trades?
  - any small business?

- **Geographic scope**
  - local-first?
  - statewide?
  - nationwide?

- **Ideal lead signals**
  - no website
  - outdated website
  - poor SEO
  - weak branding
  - no clear CTA
  - low reviews / weak presence
  - broken mobile experience
  - no social presence

- **Data fields the skill should collect**
  Example:
  - business name
  - website
  - location
  - phone
  - email
  - Google profile/socials
  - notes
  - why they’re a fit
  - lead score
  - recommended offer angle

- **Scoring system**
  We should define what makes a lead:
  - high value
  - fast close
  - likely to need Xpose Market
  - likely able to pay

- **Output format**
  Should the skill produce:
  - a markdown table?
  - CSV-style output?
  - CRM-ready fields?
  - a ranked top-10 list?
  - outreach-ready lead cards?

- **What the skill should not do**
  Important so it stays reliable.
  Example:
  - don’t hallucinate missing contact info
  - don’t mark someone “high fit” without evidence
  - don’t claim website problems unless observed
