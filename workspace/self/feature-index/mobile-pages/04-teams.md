# Mobile Teams

Routes: `#mobile/teams` and `#mobile/teams/<teamId>[/<tab>]`.

The overview shows team count/grid, refresh, and a featured-team preview with members, workspace and recent-run progress. The detail page presents Start Run, Pause/Resume, Review and Delete controls plus six tabs:

- **Context:** purpose, current task/goal, member states, active dispatches and context-reference cards.
- **Subagents:** team members.
- **Workspace:** team-scoped files.
- **Memory:** team memory/context view.
- **Runs:** team run records.
- **Team Chat:** streamed collaboration chat.

All actions operate through the same team manager/member gateway model as desktop. The phone does not flatten a team into one chat or directly rewrite completed member runs.
