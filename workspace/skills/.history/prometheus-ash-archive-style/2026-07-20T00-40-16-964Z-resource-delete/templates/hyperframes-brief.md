# Ash & Archive HyperFrames Brief

## Anti-AI art direction

This will feel like an archival field manual for a local intelligence command center, not a purple-blue AI SaaS promo.

## Narrative pattern

1. Black boot / wake phrase / tiny ember mark.
2. Evidence of local operation: files, browser, desktop, memory, tool routes.
3. Dossier mode: off-white document UI, task rows, thin rules, approval stamp.
4. Scale: teams, schedules, automations, creative artifacts, external apps.
5. Human stakes: one operating layer for work/life, not another chatbot tab.
6. Final quiet mark: Prometheus as the living local command center.

## Required visible proof

- At least one file path or local route.
- At least one memory/tool/team/status trace.
- At least one approval or artifact proof mark.
- At least one real UI/process-inspired panel.

## Motion vocabulary

Use document reveals, mechanical wipes, stamped labels, cursor/log ticks, shutter cuts, row-by-row table activation, and measured camera pans. Avoid random float/pulse/glow.
