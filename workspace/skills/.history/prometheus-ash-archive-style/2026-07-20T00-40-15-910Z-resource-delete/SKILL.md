# Prometheus One Brand System

Use this skill for Prometheus-branded visual, interface, promo, HyperFrames, website, mobile, release, or asset work. It supersedes the former Ash & Archive / charcoal-and-ember default direction for all new work.

## Core identity

Prometheus One is a sovereign, precise local AI command center: premium, calm, cinematic, and engineered. It should feel like a finished operating system rather than a cyberpunk dashboard, a distressed field manual, or generic AI SaaS.

The signature identity is the **P1 gold ring and star mark**, placed with room to breathe. Use real product UI, real tools, tasks, memory, approvals, teams, schedules, artifacts, and local-computer process proof rather than decorative pseudo-HUD copy.

## Visual system

Use the bundled [Prometheus One palette](palettes/prometheus-one.md).

- Foundation: true black and near-black surfaces with deliberate negative space.
- Accent: restrained metallic gold for identity, primary action, selected state, focus, and important progress or status.
- Supporting neutrals: warm bone/off-white typography and muted bronze borders/details.
- Geometry: sparse constellation points, orbital arcs, ring forms, celestial alignment, and precision grid logic.
- Typography: refined display serif only for major brand/editorial moments; crisp readable UI sans for interfaces; monospace only for genuine technical traces.
- Surface treatment: solid, matte, tactile, and crisp. Subtle gold bloom or film grain is permitted only when it supports legibility and hierarchy.

## Explicit prohibitions

- No default purple, blue, or cyan AI gradients.
- No blanket gold recoloring. Gold earns attention through restraint.
- No ash/ember/orange as the default system; ember may appear only as a rare intentional fire-energy accent.
- No oxblood/paper-grain/scanline/stamp degradation as the defining language.
- No generic glassmorphism, floating glows, dense fake terminal text, or rounded cards around every object.
- Do not redraw, distort, or replace the approved P1 mark with AI-generated approximations.

## Motion

Motion is slow, deliberate, and structural: orbital alignment, restrained ring rotation, gold tracing, precise section reveals, decisive cuts, and readable holds. Do not make every element drift, pulse, or glow. Preserve sufficient dwell time for on-screen text and product proof.

## Delivery standard

Before delivery, render/export and inspect representative frames or live UI states. Confirm readable text, real product/process proof, correct P1 asset fidelity, black-first hierarchy, and selective gold use. For HyperFrames, pair this skill with `hyperframes` / `hyperframes-creative` as needed.
