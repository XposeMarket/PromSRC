# Ash & Archive Palette

Avoid default blue/purple gradients. Use fewer colors and make every accent mean something.

## Core tokens

- Obsidian: `#070706` — cinematic black, command/boot space.
- Charcoal: `#151411` — panel black, dark UI surface.
- Ash: `#2b2924` — rules, quiet borders, dark secondary material.
- Bone: `#eee7d6` — paper/dossier background.
- Paper: `#d8cfbb` — muted cards and document fields.
- Dust: `#8f8777` — secondary labels and aged print.
- Ember: `#ff5a1f` — Prometheus fire/status/action accent.
- Oxblood: `#7b1e2b` — warnings, approval stamps, critical proof.
- Machine Green: `#9fbf70` — completed task/live signal.
- Brass: `#c6a15b` — rare mythic/heritage accent.

## Use rules

- Dominant frame color should usually be Obsidian or Bone.
- Ember is a signature accent, not a full-screen wash.
- Oxblood is for stamps, warnings, denials, proof marks, and irreversible gates.
- Machine Green means live/completed/operational.
- Brass is rare: seals, title glyphs, legacy/myth references.
- No full-screen blue/purple/cyan gradients.
- If a dark scene needs depth, use grain, typography, rules, and localized ember light instead of neon glow.
