# Revenue-system handoff

After qualification, emit CRM-ready evidence records rather than only a lead list.

| Business/lead | Qualification event | Evidence source | Confidence | Next best revenue action | Owner | Due date | Approval state | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---:|---|---|---|---|---|

The next best action must name the intervention (research, audit, draft outreach, call, follow-up, disqualify), owner, due date, and approval boundary. External sends and CRM mutations remain approval-gated.

Close the loop after replies, calls, wins, losses, or disqualification. Record outcome and which qualification signals were predictive or misleading, then revise future scoring rules from evidence.

For funnel-wide module design, authoritative systems, measurement, and approval matrices, use `revenue-agent-system-designer`.
