# Financial models

Create an auditable chain from assumptions to outputs.

1. Confirm decision, horizon, granularity, currency, accounting conventions, scenarios, and required outputs.
2. Separate assumptions, calculations, and presentation.
3. Model operational drivers before financial totals.
4. Link revenue, costs, headcount, working capital, capex, financing, cash flow, and balance-sheet effects as relevant.
5. Add base/upside/downside scenarios and sensitivities around uncertain drivers.
6. Include checks for balance, sign, units, period alignment, circularity, and cash reconciliation.
7. Document sources and highlight assumptions requiring owner approval.

Never hide hard-coded values inside formulas or present forecasts as facts. Verify workbook formulas and representative outputs after generation.
