## 4. Transformation Result
**Use when:** the user has a strong before/after, measurable outcome, or identity shift.
**Best for:** productivity wins, client growth, lifestyle changes, business proof.
**Instruction:** expand this category custom for the user's niche. The point is the **dramatic end state**, not the exact template.

Starter templates:
1. I quit my 9-5 after using this for 30 days...
2. This helped me read 50 books in 3 months...
3. I stopped doom scrolling completely with this...
4. This helped me land 100+ clients in 30 days...
5. I turned $500 into $10K using this app...
6. This cured my procrastination permanently...

**Build formula:**
- `I went from [before] to [after] because [mechanism]`
- `This helped me [specific win] in [specific time]`
- `How I [dream outcome] without [common pain]`
