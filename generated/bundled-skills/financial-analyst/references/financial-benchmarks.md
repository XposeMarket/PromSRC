# Financial Benchmarks by Industry & Stage

## SaaS Benchmarks
| Metric | Early Stage | Growth | Scale |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gross Margin | 60-70% | 70-80% | 75-85% |
| MoM Growth | 15-25% | 8-15% | 3-8% |
| Net Churn | <5% annual | <3% annual | <2% annual |
| NRR | >100% | >110% | >120% |
| LTV:CAC | >2:1 | >3:1 | >5:1 |
| CAC Payback | <24 mo | <18 mo | <12 mo |
| S&M % of Revenue | 40-60% | 30-50% | 20-35% |
| R&D % of Revenue | 25-40% | 20-30% | 15-25% |

## E-commerce Benchmarks
| Metric | Benchmark |
|---|---|
| Gross Margin | 30-50% |
| AOV | Depends on category ($30-$300 typical) |
| Conversion Rate | 1-3% (website) |
| Cart Abandonment | 65-75% (industry average) |
| Customer Repeat Rate | 25-40% (healthy) |
| Email Revenue % | 20-30% of total revenue |
| ROAS (paid) | >3x to be sustainable |

## Services / Agency Benchmarks
| Metric | Benchmark |
|---|---|
| Gross Margin | 40-65% |
| Utilization Rate | 70-80% (billable hours / total hours) |
| Revenue per Employee | $100-200k (healthy) |
| Net Promoter Score | >40 |
| Client Retention | >85% annual |

## Fundraising Valuation Multiples (as of 2024-2025)
| Stage | ARR Multiple |
|---|---|
| Pre-seed | N/A (team + idea) |
| Seed | 8-15x ARR (if any) |
| Series A | 6-12x ARR |
| Series B | 5-10x ARR |
| Series C+ | 4-8x ARR |
| Profitable/bootstrapped | 3-6x ARR |

Note: Multiples compressed significantly from 2021 peaks. Profitability path matters more now.
