# SDR sales

Build a measurable motion from target account to qualified opportunity.

1. Define ICP, buyer roles, disqualifiers, offer, proof, geography, and sales capacity.
2. Map stages with explicit entry/exit criteria and ownership.
3. Define research fields and qualification evidence before outreach.
4. Design a multi-channel sequence with a distinct purpose for each touch.
5. Create discovery questions that test problem, impact, timing, authority, and fit without turning the call into an interrogation.
6. Specify CRM fields, handoffs, follow-up SLA, and weekly metrics.
7. Review conversion by stage and change one bottleneck at a time.

Never invent personalization, contacts, intent signals, or customer proof. Do not send outreach without authorization. Use `local-lead-hunting` for discovery and `cold-outreach-writer` for message-level copy.

Read [detailed-guide.md](references/detailed-guide.md) for scripts, scorecards, cadence templates, objection handling, metrics, and playbook formats. Read [sales-metrics.md](references/sales-metrics.md) only when defining the dashboard.
