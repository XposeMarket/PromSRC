# SDR Benchmarks & Sales KPIs

## SDR Activity Benchmarks (per rep, per day)
| Activity | Low | Average | High Performer |
|---|---|---|---|
| Emails sent | 30 | 50 | 80+ |
| LinkedIn touches | 10 | 20 | 30+ |
| Calls made | 20 | 40 | 60+ |
| New prospects added | 10 | 20 | 30+ |

## Conversion Benchmarks
| Metric | Benchmark |
|---|---|
| Email open rate (cold) | 15-25% |
| Email reply rate (cold) | 2-8% |
| Reply → meeting booked | 20-40% |
| Meeting → qualified opp | 40-60% |
| Qualified opp → close | 20-35% |
| Overall outbound lead → close | 0.5-3% |

## SDR Quota Benchmarks
| Deal Size | Meetings per Month | Pipeline per Month |
|---|---|---|
| SMB ($5-20k ACV) | 15-25 meetings | $150-300k pipeline |
| Mid-market ($20-100k ACV) | 8-15 meetings | $300-600k pipeline |
| Enterprise ($100k+ ACV) | 3-8 meetings | $500k-2M pipeline |

## Outreach Response Rates by Channel
| Channel | Response Rate |
|---|---|
| Cold email (generic) | 1-3% |
| Cold email (personalized) | 5-15% |
| LinkedIn InMail | 10-25% |
| Cold call (connected) | 2-5% |
| Warm intro | 40-70% |
| Referral | 50-80% |

## Follow-up Statistics
- 80% of deals require 5+ follow-up touches
- 44% of reps give up after 1 follow-up
- Persistence (5-8 touches) closes the majority of outbound deals
- Best follow-up timing: 3 days, 7 days, 14 days, 30 days
