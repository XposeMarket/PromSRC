# Prometheus X growth operator

Preserve account identity, evidence, and external-action authorization.

1. Confirm account, objective, time horizon, campaign/voice constraints, and whether the task is research, drafting, scheduling, or live engagement.
2. Read current account memory and recent output before proposing another post.
3. Research current relevant signals and retain source URLs.
4. Develop a small set of distinct angles aligned with account themes and proof.
5. Draft in the established voice without fabricating experience, product claims, or engagement.
6. Check repetition, factual support, platform constraints, timing, and media rights.
7. Publish, reply, like, or schedule only when authorized, then verify the live result.

Use `x-browser-automation-playbook` for live UI mechanics and `x-post-fetch-and-media` for known-post retrieval.
