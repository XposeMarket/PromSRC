These are examples, not fixed copy. Rewrite against live context before posting.

## Product Thesis

> Chatbots wait for prompts.
> Prometheus opens the app, does the work, remembers what happened, and comes back tomorrow better.

> The next useful AI product probably won’t feel like a better search box.
> It’ll feel like your computer finally has an operator.

> Most AI tools stop at the answer.
> Prometheus is being built for everything after the answer: apps, files, approvals, memory, follow-up.

## Building in Public

> Today Prometheus learned a new X workflow, tested it live, saved it as a reusable composite, and updated its own playbook afterward.
> That loop matters more than the individual feature.

> The hard part of agents isn’t making them click buttons.
> The hard part is making them recover, remember what worked, and not become dangerous when they get confident.

## Anti-Generic AI

> I don’t want another tab with a smarter text box.
> I want an assistant that can operate the machine.

> If your “agent” can’t check the screen, use the browser, remember the result, and follow up later, it’s probably still just chat with a costume on.

## Business Use Case

> Small businesses don’t need another dashboard.
> They need one place that can read the inbox, check the calendar, draft the quote, look up the part, and ask before sending the invoice.

> The real AI opportunity for local businesses is not “content at scale.”
> It’s operational relief.
